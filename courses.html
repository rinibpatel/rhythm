Professional Course

The Professional Courses at the Rhythm academy of Music are aimed at musicians, producers, sound engineers and DJ’s who are looking to make a career in music. Each course has been developed with industry relevance in mind and with the objective of helping students prepare for a commercial career. At Rhythm School we have a range of study options to suityour aspirations. Courses are made up of fixed modules with the option of electives.

BASS

The Pro Guitar classes are aimed at creating a versatile performer. The objective of these courses is to learn different genres of music like Rock, Blues, Funk, etc., that enables a musician to use these skills both in sessions playing or on the stage. An understanding of these styles not only helps students in pursuing independent music but also commercial Bollywood and non Bollywood music.

MUSIC PRODUCTION

The Pro Music Production classes are aimed at creating Music Producers who can create music for a wide variety of formats including films, radio, television, games, independent music. etc. Unlike traditional music production courses offered in the country today, we aim at not just developing our students technical skills on music softwares, but also their creative and composition skills with regards to song writing

JUNIOR COURSE

The Pro Junior course is perfectly tailored for youngsters who are interested in an active,in-depth experience of music. Students who are inclined towards pursuing their musical education beyond the satisfaction of just a hobby in piano, guitar or singing, will be taught comprehensive theory and practical knowledge through this age-appropriate, systematic course. For this purpose, they will have to undergo an assessment.

DJ

The Pro DJ Classes at School are aimed at creating DJ’s who not only want to spin music in different formats,but also want to cross over into electronic music production.We cover formats that include Vinyl,CD’s and Digital Music (Traktor, Ableton & Maschine).Our institute experienced DJ faculty teaching a world class curriculum, ensures that our students have what it takes to make it, in the electronic music world.
